Research

What I learned while building the electronics app.

Electronics research summary

I studied user behavior around electronic waste, planned app features to make recycling easier, and created a simple interface for students and families.

  • Identified key user needs: easy disposal, local recycling info, and environmental impact awareness.
  • Designed a mobile-first app flow for quick guidance and clear choices.
  • Documented research findings and translated them into usable app screens.

Research Document

My electronics recycling app project brings together research, user-centered design, and practical app development. This document shows the study, features, and lessons learned while creating a product that helps people make better recycling decisions.

Project Goal

Build a simple, student-friendly app that makes it easy to understand and act on electronic waste recycling options.

What I Studied
  • How people currently dispose of old electronics.
  • What stops users from recycling electronics responsibly.
  • Which app features would help users make better decisions quickly.
App Features I Designed
  • A clean home screen with clear steps for recycling electronics.
  • Location-based pickup and drop-off guidance.
  • Educational cards explaining environmental impact and safe disposal.
What I Learned
  • Research improves design by focusing on real user needs.
  • Building apps means balancing useful features with easy, fast interactions.
  • I want to continue learning AI and app development to build tools that solve real problems.
