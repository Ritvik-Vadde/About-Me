06 — Research

What the research actually taught me.

I studied how people currently handle electronic waste, mapped the barriers to using recycling centers, and designed a simple interface for students and families to act on what they learned.

  • Identified key user needs: easy disposal, local info, environmental impact awareness.
  • Designed a mobile-first flow for fast, clear choices.
  • Translated research findings into usable app screens.

Project goal

Build a simple, student-friendly app that makes it easy to understand and act on electronic waste recycling options.

What I studied

  • How people currently dispose of old electronics.
  • What stops users from recycling electronics responsibly.
  • Which app features would help users make better decisions quickly.

App features I designed

  • A clean home screen with clear steps for recycling electronics.
  • Location-based pickup and drop-off guidance.
  • Educational cards explaining environmental impact and safe disposal.

What I learned

  • Research improves design by focusing on real user needs.
  • Building apps means balancing useful features with fast, easy interactions.
  • I want to keep learning AI and app development to build tools that solve real problems.
