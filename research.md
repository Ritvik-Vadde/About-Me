# Electronics Recycling App Research

## Project goal
Build an app that helps students and families better understand electronics recycling, find local recycling resources, and make responsible disposal choices.

## What I studied
- How people currently dispose of old electronics
- The barriers to using recycling centers
- What information users need to feel confident about e-waste disposal

## App features I designed
- A simple home screen with quick steps for recycling electronics
- Local pickup and drop-off guidance for e-waste
- Information cards explaining why recycling electronics matters

## What I learned
- Research helps me make clearer user flows and stronger interface decisions
- Building an app means balancing design, accessibility, and functionality
- I enjoy working on projects that combine programming and real-world impact
